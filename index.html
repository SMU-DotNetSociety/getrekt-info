What is GetRekt

Get Rekt is a series of coding challenges that are made to push your skills to the limit. It will have questions of escalating difficulty to challenge your coding ability. So come on and sign up for the challenge. Attractive Prizes will be awarded. It will be a good practice for your lab test.
